What's on the floor

Built for the work,
not the selfie.

No fluff, no crowd-management gimmicks — a straightforward floor plan with the equipment people actually come back for.

24/7 Access

Badge in whenever your shift ends. The floor doesn't close, so your training doesn't have to fit someone else's schedule.

Turf Sprint Lane

A yard-marked conditioning lane for sled pushes, sprints and finishers — laid straight down the middle of the gym.

Free Weights & Machines

Dumbbells from 4kg to 55kg, plate-loaded rigs and selectorized machines, racked and labeled for quick changeovers.

Coached one-to-one sessions on the same floor you train on — no separate studio, no scheduling runaround.

Protein, creatine and pre-workout stocked at reception, so post-session recovery doesn't need a second stop.

Cardio Row
